Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: June 2025

🔒 Privacy Policy

MapOfSounds ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We store this locally in your browser to keep you logged in.
  • Uploaded Content: When you upload a sound, we collect the audio file, title, description, location coordinates, and tags you provide.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data such as play counts, likes, and page views to improve the service.
  • Comments: When you post a comment, the text and your display name are stored alongside the sound.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the MapOfSounds platform.
  • To display your uploaded sounds on the interactive map.
  • To personalize your experience (favorites, play history, theme preference).
  • To prevent abuse and verify uploads via Cloudflare Turnstile.
  • To authenticate users via Google Sign-In.

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Preferences (theme, favorites, play history, login session) are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you upload content.
  • Cloud Storage: Uploaded audio files are stored in Cloudflare R2. Sound metadata is stored in Cloudflare KV.
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Identity Services: Used for sign-in. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile: Used for bot protection on uploads. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Mapbox: Used for map rendering. Subject to Mapbox's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Uploaded sounds and associated metadata (title, description, location, tags) are publicly visible on the platform.

6. Your Rights

  • You can sign out at any time to remove your session from the browser.
  • You can clear your localStorage to remove all local preferences and history.
  • To request deletion of uploaded content, contact us at mapofsound@rukkit.net.

7. Children's Privacy

MapOfSounds is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
